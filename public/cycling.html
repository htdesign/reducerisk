Ash's Story

“One split decision by someone else changed my life. I left work and headed home biking along the cycleway down Te Rapa Straight. A car parked on the cycleway opened his door right on me, and then an oncoming car going past ran me over.”

Ash's story is a reminder to all drivers that you don't just have to watch out for other road users while you are moving.
